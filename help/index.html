How it Works

1

Submit a Claim

Fill out the missing marks form with your unit details and the name of the lecturer in charge of that course.

2

Receive Reference ID

Once submitted, you will get a unique Reference ID. Keep this safe to track your progress or use the View page to confirm your submissions.

3

Department Review

Your claim is sent to the respective department where the lecturer confirms your attendance and exam record.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process a claim?
Processing depends on the availability of the lecturer and internal departmental checks.
What is a "Reference ID"?
It is a unique code generated for every submission. You can use it in the "View Claims" section to see the status of your report.
